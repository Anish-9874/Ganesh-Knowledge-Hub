Official News

Date: September 5, 2025

The Government of Nepal has announced a temporary suspension of several social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter due to national security concerns.

Authorities have stated that the platforms will remain inaccessible until further notice. Users are advised to follow official communication channels for updates.

Ganesh Knowledge Hub assures that all platforms will be fully available again immediately after the official unban, and users can continue to enjoy social media services safely and securely.
